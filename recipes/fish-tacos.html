Fish Tacos




recipe link

Description

Fish Tacos- perfectly seasoned white fish served in tortillas with a refreshing cabbage
slaw, lime crema, pickled red onions, avocado, and cotija cheese. You only need 30
minutes to make these fresh and flavorful fish tacos, they are a weeknight favorite.


These Fish Tacos are also a crowd pleaser. They only take 30 minutes to make and taste
better than any fish tacos you can order at a restaurant. They are fresh, light, and
full of flavor! The fish is seasoned with a simple homemade taco seasoning made with
chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and garlic powder. Cook the fish in a hot cast
iron skillet until golden, tender, and flaky.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs cod (or other white fish like tilapia or halibut)
  • Juice of 1 large lime, 2 tablespoons
  • 1 TBSP chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
